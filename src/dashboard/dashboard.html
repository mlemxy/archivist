How to get your cookies

  1. Open archiveofourown.org in Chrome while logged in.
  2. Right-click anywhere and choose Inspect.
  3. Go to the Application tab.
  4. Expand Cookies in the sidebar and click https://archiveofourown.org.
  5. Copy the Value of each cookie into the fields below.
These cookies cannot be read via the browser console (HttpOnly). Copy them from the Application tab only. _otwarchive_session is the only required one.
